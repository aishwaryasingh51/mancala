How to Play Mancala

üéØ Objective

Collect more stones in your store than your opponent.

üéÆ Gameplay

  • Click on any of your pits (bottom row) to pick up all stones
  • Distribute stones counter-clockwise, one per pit
  • Include your store but skip opponent's store
  • If your last stone lands in your store, take another turn
  • If your last stone lands in an empty pit on your side, capture that stone plus all stones in the opposite pit

üèÅ Game End

When all pits on one side are empty, the game ends. The other player captures all remaining stones. The player with the most stones wins!
